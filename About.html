Clash Of Clans

About CLash Of Clans

At its core, Clash of Clans — available from Google Play — is a strategy game whose primary objective is to be the person with the most trophies. You earn trophies either by successfully attacking someone else's village, or by successfully defending against an attack from another online player.

The game is heavily promoted. In February 2015, Supercell released their Clash of Clans Super Bowl XLIX commercial, featuring Liam Neeson parodying his character from Taken. On February 2, Business Insider reported the ad as the 5th most watched Super Bowl ad, though on February 6, VentureBeat reported the ad was the most viewed of those that appeared on the Super Bowl.[32] YouTube users later voted the advertisement the second best Super Bowl ad, behind Nissan's "With Dad" ad.
